About

Made for anyone who loves being outside

Fynbos & Flame is a placeholder name for a project built around the trails, coastline and forests of the Garden Route — a small corner of South Africa with an outsized amount of wild ground to cover. Whatever brings you outdoors, whether that's the quiet, the challenge, or the sense of something bigger than yourself out there, you're welcome here.
